# Custom Ordering: Understanding the 3-Tier System

_Verified by Elizabeth & Nicole_

# Introduction: Your Guide to H&C's Custom Ordering Programs

**Purpose of This Guide:** This guide serves as both an orientation program for new team members and an ongoing reference resource for information about Hair & Compounds' custom ordering systems. Whether you're helping a customer understand their options, processing an order, or explaining our capabilities, this document provides the essential knowledge you need.
Hair & Compounds offers three distinct pathways for custom hair extensions, each designed for different customer needs and business scales:
*   **Tier 1 (CUSTOM)**: Per-client custom orders
*   **Tier 2 (PRIVATE LABEL)**: Signature brand lines
*   **Tier 3 (WHITE LABEL/WHOLESALE)**: Bulk wholesale distribution
Understanding these three tiers enables you to guide customers to the right solution for their goals, whether they're stylists ordering for individual clients, professionals building their own branded line, or entrepreneurs establishing wholesale businesses.

* * *

# Tier 1: CUSTOM ORDERING (Per Client)

### What It Is
Custom ordering allows you to design hair extensions for individual clients with specifications beyond our standard stock offerings. Perfect for clients who need precise color matching, unique blends, specific densities, or special lengths.
### Who It's For
**Customer Profile:**
*   Stylists ordering for specific client needs
*   One-time special event extensions
*   Clients with hard-to-match hair colors or textures
*   Testing custom designs before committing to larger orders
**When to Recommend Tier 1:** Team members should guide customers to Tier 1 when they need quick customization for individual clients, have small quantity needs, or want to test a design before scaling up.
### Requirements
*   **Pro-Access Account**: Required
*   **Minimum Order**: 4 bundles (Poly/Cylinder) or 4 ounces (Hand-Tied Wefts)
*   **Order History**: Not required (but Color Swatch Set recommended)
*   **Agreement**: None required
### Pricing & Timeline
*   **Cost**: 25-30% more than stock prices
*   **Timeline**: 4-5 weeks production
*   **Deposit**: 50% required upfront
*   **Non-Refundable**: All custom orders are final
### Design Flexibility
**Colors:**
*   Any combination from 45 colors including grey white
*   Color matching from samples you send
*   Multidimensional blends: balayage, ombre, highlights, color melts
*   Custom rooted combinations
**Textures & Finishes:**
*   Natural wave to tight ringlet curls (100% natural, as grown)
*   Full thick hair or layered with naturally grown shorter lengths
*   Choose how hair begins and ends for perfect blending
**Lengths:**
*   Any length from 10" to 30" (or longer with advance sourcing)
*   Temple-direct sourcing for specialty lengths
**Densities:**
*   All 6 weft density options available (see Weft Density Sample Set)
*   Heavier strand weights for individuals
*   Custom track densities
### The Custom Ordering Process
*   **Step 1: Design Your Extension** Submit design via online form (Weft, Cylinder, or Poly form). Specify all details: color, texture, length, quantity, density, finish.
*   **Step 2: Receive Quote** Get quote for cost and delivery date. Revise design if needed until satisfied.
*   **Step 3: Pay Deposit** Accept estimate and pay 50% deposit via payment link. Order begins production once payment received.
*   **Step 4: Watch Production** Receive production confirmation with order number and Custom Portal link to monitor production stages.
*   **Step 5: Quality Inspection** Upon arrival at H&C Los Angeles warehouse, extensions undergo rigorous quality control:
    *       *   Check color, texture, length, finish root to tip
        *   Wash and tangle test
        *   Condition and air dry to confirm natural texture
        *   Final approval before shipment
*   **Step 6: Delivery** Receive final invoice with payment link. Once paid in full, order ships with tracking information.
### Key Advantages
*   **Time Savings**: No need to modify stock extensions with roots, lowlights, or color adjustments
*   **Perfect Match**: Extensions arrive ready for installation
*   **Low Minimum**: Test custom designs without large investment
*   **Quality Assurance**: H&C handles all quality control in Los Angeles

# Tier 2: PRIVATE LABEL (Signature Line)

### What It Is
Private Label allows you to create your own branded line of hair extensions with recurring designs. Build an exclusive product line that clients can only get from you, establishing your unique brand identity in the market.
### Who It's For
**Customer Profile:**
*   Established stylists building their brand
*   Salons wanting exclusive product lines
*   Extension specialists with repeat color/texture needs
*   Professionals ready to offer signature products
**When to Recommend Tier 2:** Team members should guide customers to Private Label when they have recurring design needs, want brand exclusivity, meet the $5,000 minimum threshold, and have established order history with H&C.
### Requirements
*   **Pro-Access Account**: Required
*   **Signed Agreement**: Private Label Agreement required
*   **Minimum Order**: $5,000 per order
*   **Order History**: 6 previous orders OR payment via bank wire/Zelle
### Pricing & Timeline
*   **Cost**: 10% discount from custom prices
*   **Timeline**: 5-7 weeks production
*   **Deposit**: 50% required upfront
*   **Non-Refundable**: All orders are final
### The Private Label Process
*   **Step 1: Review & Sign Agreement** Agreement outlines ordering process, payment terms, delivery timeline, and return policy.
*   **Step 2: Design Your Extension** Fill out Private Label order form with complete specifications for your signature line.
*   **Step 3: Receive Quote** Get quote for cost and delivery date. Adjust design as needed.
*   **Step 4: Pay Deposit** Accept estimate and pay 50% deposit. Order placed upon payment receipt.
*   **Step 5: Watch Production** Receive confirmation with order number and Custom Portal access.
*   **Step 6: Quality Inspection** H&C performs comprehensive quality control in Los Angeles: color verification, texture check, wash/tangle test, conditioning, air drying.
*   **Step 7: Delivery** Receive final invoice, pay remaining 50%, receive shipment with tracking.
### Strategic Benefits
**Brand Exclusivity:**
*   Create signature line exclusive to your business
*   Differentiate from competitors using generic brands
*   Build customer loyalty through proprietary products
**Customization Control:**
*   Design multiple color/texture combinations in your line
*   Curate offerings specific to your clientele
*   Adjust line over time based on demand
**Business Growth:**
*   Low-risk way to establish branded product line
*   Grow inventory strategically without excess stock
*   Scale brand visibility and reputation
**Supply Chain Simplicity:**
*   Work only with H&C American team
*   No overseas manufacturer coordination
*   H&C handles duties, taxes, global logistics
*   Quality control performed in Los Angeles
### Private Label vs. White Label
**Private Label (Tier 2):**
*   Purchase as-needed while building brand
*   Market directly to your clients
*   Lower minimums, more flexibility
*   Ships from Los Angeles
**White Label (Tier 3):**
*   Bulk buying for wholesale distribution
*   Sell to other businesses/salons
*   Higher minimums, better discounts
*   Ships directly from India

# Tier 3: WHITE LABEL/WHOLESALE

### What It Is
White Label is for businesses purchasing large volumes directly from our production house (Different Hair Pvt Ltd) in Chennai, India. You become a wholesaler, buying in bulk and distributing to other salons, stylists, or retail channels.
### Who It's For
**Customer Profile:**
*   Hair extension distributors
*   Large salon chains
*   Retailers selling to multiple locations
*   Entrepreneurs entering wholesale hair business
**When to Recommend Tier 3:** Team members should guide customers to White Label when they plan to resell (not just use personally), can handle $10,000+ minimums, have import/customs expertise, and are comfortable coordinating directly with our India production house. Note: This tier requires significant international logistics knowledge—ensure customers understand their responsibilities.
### Requirements
*   **Pro-Access Account**: Required
*   **Signed Agreement**: Wholesale Agreement required
*   **Minimum Order**: $10,000 per order
*   **Order History**: 6 previous orders required
### Pricing & Timeline
*   **Cost**: 10% discount from stock prices (starting rate)
*   **Timeline**: 8-10 weeks production
*   **Deposit**: 50% required upfront via wire transfer
*   **Non-Refundable**: All orders are final
### The Wholesale Process
*   **Step 1: Review & Sign Agreement** Wholesale agreement covers ordering, payment, delivery, shipping/customs, and returns. More complex than Tier 1-2 due to international shipping responsibilities.
*   **Step 2: Design Your Extension** Complete wholesale order form with full specifications. Larger quantities typically require more detailed planning.
*   **Step 3: Receive Proforma Invoice** Different Hair Pvt Ltd (DHPL) sends email with total cost from India.
*   **Step 4: Wire Deposit** Confirm proforma invoice and wire 50% deposit directly to DHPL in India.
*   **Step 5: Production Process** Communicate directly with DHPL throughout production. Larger quantities require 6-8+ weeks depending on order size.
*   **Step 6: Final Payment** Receive notification when order is complete. Pay remaining 50% balance to DHPL.
*   **Step 7: Shipment** DHPL ships directly from India. You receive tracking information.
*   **Step 8: Import & InspectionCritical**: You are responsible for:
    *   Importing extensions into United States
    *   All customs paperwork and processes
    *   Duty fees and import taxes
    *   Unpacking and inspecting shipment
    *   Verifying order contents against invoice
### Key Considerations
*   **Import Responsibilities:** Unlike Tiers 1-2 where H&C handles all logistics, Tier 3 requires you to manage international shipping, customs clearance, and duty payments. Factor these costs and processes into your business model.
*   **Direct Production Communication:** You work directly with Different Hair Pvt Ltd in India, not the H&C Los Angeles team. Time zone differences and international communication should be considered.
*   **Volume Requirements:** $10,000 minimums mean significant upfront capital and storage capacity needed. Ensure you have infrastructure to receive, store, and distribute large quantities.
*   **Extended Timeline:** 8-10+ weeks allows for bulk production but requires advance planning for inventory needs.

# Common Across All Tiers

### Ethical Sourcing
All three tiers use the same 100% Indian Remy hair, ethically sourced from temples in Southern India. Hair is cuticle-intact, running in the same direction, processed under ISO 22716 standards.
### Quality Standards
Whether ordering 4 bundles or $10,000 wholesale, hair quality remains identical. Production follows the same strict protocols ensuring consistent results.
### Non-Refundable Policy
All custom, private label, and wholesale orders are 100% non-refundable. Careful planning and clear specifications are essential before placing deposits.
### Design Flexibility
All tiers offer the same customization options for colors, textures, lengths, and densities. The difference is in order size, pricing, and logistics handling.

# For H&C Team Members: Customer Support Guidelines

### Common Questions & Answers
**"What's the difference between custom and private label?"** Custom (Tier 1) is for individual client orders with low minimums. Private Label (Tier 2) is for building your own branded line with higher minimums and better pricing. Custom is per-client; Private Label is for recurring business.
**"Can I start with Tier 2 or 3 without order history?"** Tier 2 requires 6 previous orders OR payment via bank wire/Zelle. Tier 3 requires 6 previous orders (no exceptions). New customers should start with Tier 1.
**"Why is there a 50% deposit?"** All custom work is non-refundable because it's made specifically to customer specifications. The deposit commits both parties and covers initial production costs.
**"Can we rush production?"** No. Quality custom work requires proper production time: 4-5 weeks (Tier 1), 5-7 weeks (Tier 2), 8-10 weeks (Tier 3). Do not promise faster timelines.
**"What if the customer doesn't like it?"** All custom orders are non-refundable. Emphasize careful specification review before ordering. This is why getting quotes and confirming designs is critical.
### Key Reminders
1. **Always verify Pro-Access account** before discussing custom options
2. **Confirm order history** before offering Tier 2 or 3
3. **Set realistic timeline expectations** - under-promise, over-deliver
4. **Emphasize non-refundable policy** multiple times during sales process
5. **Document all specifications** in writing before order placement
6. **Use Custom Portal** link to keep customers informed during production
