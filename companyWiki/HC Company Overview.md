# **About Hair & Compounds & its Mission**

Hair & Compounds has operated in the hair extension industry for over 30 years. We specialize exclusively in 100% natural Indian Remy hair extensions.

Our mission is to source, preserve, and deliver **exceptional-quality, ethically sourced natural hair** while educating the industry on how to work with it responsibly. We commit to:
*       *   Sourcing 100% ethical Southern Indian temple hair
    *   Preserving the hair’s natural cuticle, texture, and movement
    *   Refusing chemical shortcuts such as perming or artificial texturizing
    *   Creating hair that lasts years, not washes
    *   Educating stylists, teams, and clients on texture, care, and integrity
    *   Designing products that prioritize hair health, balance, and realism
    *   Innovation through our unique R&D department dedicated to hair science

Through transparency, education, and uncompromising standards, Hair Compounds empowers professionals to create results that honor both the hair and the person wearing it.

# **Core Principles**

1. **Natural Hair Only:** We never use chemicals to create textures. All our textures come from naturally occurring hair patterns.
2. **Ethical Sourcing:** We source 100% of our hair from Southern temples in India. The hair is donated by temple visitors. We maintain direct relationships with our sources.
3. **Quality Standards:** We preserve the hair cuticle on every strand. We never strip the outer layer. This makes our hair more expensive to produce but ensures superior quality and longevity.
4. **Customer Education:** We believe informed customers make better decisions. We provide extensive education about hair types, care, and realistic expectations.
5. **Transparency:** We are honest about our processes, limitations, and natural variations in hair. We never make false claims about our products.

# **What We Offer**

### **Hair Textures**
We specialize in textured hair only. We offer three natural texture types.
1. **Natural Wave:** Classic S-shaped waves available in two finishes. We offer both layered and premium options.
2. **Extra Body Wave:** More defined wave patterns with full body from start to finish. This texture is most versatile for blending. We offer Curly, layered and premium options.
3. **Curly:** Ringlet-forming curls that represent our tightest texture. Available in 3A and 3B curl patterns. Offered in only the layered finish.
**What we do** **NOT** **offer:** We never offer straight hair. We cannot provide Type 4 coily hair.
### **Extension Types**
We offer three professional extension application methods. Each has different characteristics, lifespan, and maintenance requirements.
1. **Cylinder I-Tips (Microlinks):** Individual strands attached with copper/brass tubes. Reusable for 1-4 years with proper care.
2. **Poly Bonds (K-Tips):** Individual strands with pre-bonded adhesive tips. Last 3-4 months and require replacement.
3. **Hand-Tied Wefts:** Hair sewn onto thin, flexible tracks. Reusable with move-ups every 6 months.
### **Color Range**
We offer colors from Level 1 (jet black) to Level 12 (platinum blonde). We provide multiple shade options including natural, ash, red, and gold tones. We also offer 6 Blends and 5 Rooted color combinations.
Specialty colors include gray white and ash white. Custom blends, rooted shades, and gray blends are available through custom orders.

Each strand is encapsulated within a clear abrasion resistant tube that will not crack or shed and can be re-attached numerous times. The tip will remain strong and flexible. After flattening during installation, the links return to their original shape when heated via a flat iron in a low heat setting. Isaac found this technology within the aerospace industry.
2. **Superior Polymer Bonds:-** Our poly bonds took ten years to perfect the formula and tip dimensions. The bond is a clear polyamide hot melt bond custom formulated for strength and endurance. It's made of Polyurethane hot melt adhesive that forms an initial bond immediately upon cooling. Our Polymer bond is easy and fast to apply with a high resistance to heat, conditioners and oils. Customers frequently request our specific bonds because of their quality. Many stylists prefer our bonds over competitors. It is NOT reusable.
3. **Cold Dye Process:-** We developed a cold dye process that reduces damage during color processing. This innovation allows us to create lighter ash tones and pure white shades, while improving the quality of all level 8-12 blond shades.
    1. The cold dye process enabled us to create Level 12 Ash and Ash White. These colors were previously impossible with standard hot dye methods.

# **What Makes Us Different from Competitors**

1. **Remy Hair Commitment :-** Many companies claim to sell Remy hair but actually strip the cuticle. We never strip the cuticle and never coat with silicone. Our hair maintains its natural structure and cuticle direction. This ensures superior quality and longevity.
2. **How to identify fake Remy hair:-** It feels like "Barbie doll hair" with a synthetic, rough texture. It may feel tacky from silicone coating. It looks good initially but degrades after 2-3 washes. It returns to straight texture after washing if chemically processed.
3. **Our genuine Remy hair:-** It feels like natural human hair with smooth, soft texture. It has no silicone coating. It maintains texture after washing and naturally returns to original pattern. It lasts 1-4 years with proper care for wefts and Cylinders. Poly bonds last 3-4 months as what outlasts the hair is the bond as it needs to be retipped after the first use if they want to reuse the hair.
4. **No Chemical Texture Creation:-** Other companies chemically process hair to create curls or waves. These textures only last a few washes before reverting to straight. Our textures are 100% natural and permanent. The hair naturally grows with these wave or curl patterns.
5. **Production Quality Control:-** We sort through kilos of hair to find high quality raw hair. We maintain strict cuticle direction standards. We reject hair that doesn't meet quality requirements. This results in a higher cost with the temple hair selections.
6. **Transparency About Limitations :-** We are honest about what we can and cannot do. We tell customers when we cannot match their texture or color. We explain natural variations and set realistic expectations. Many companies promise anything to make a sale, but we prioritize honest communication.
