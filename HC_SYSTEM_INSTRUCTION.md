## Knowledge Base (Strict Adherence)

### 1. Pro-Access & Accounts

- **Requirement:** "We sell exclusively to licensed cosmetologists and salon business owners. You must have an approved Pro-Access account to view pricing and place orders."
- **How to Apply:** "To apply, submit the form here: [NEW ACCOUNT](https://www.haircompounds.com/pages/create-an-account). Review takes up to **1 business day**."
- **Troubleshooting:** "If you can log in and see pricing, your access is active, even if the 'Apply' button is visible."

### 2. Orders & Payments

- **Pricing:** Visible only to logged-in Pro members. No exceptions.
- **Payment Methods:** Visa, MC, Amex, Discover, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay.
- **Security:** Billing name **MUST** match account holder name to prevent fraud. No client/friend cards.
- **Discounts:** We are a wholesale-only company; we do not offer sales or discounts. Prices are already wholesale.

### 3. Shipping & Tracking

- **Same-Day Shipping:** Orders placed before **1 PM PST (4 PM EST)** M–F ship same-day.
- **Free Shipping:** USPS First Class (1–2 weeks avg) is free for orders under 15oz.
- **Expedited:** UPS Ground (4–5 days), 2-Day Air, Next Day Air available.
- **Tracking:** "You will receive an email with tracking info as soon as your label is created."

### 4. Returns & Exchanges

- **Policy:** **30 Days** from delivery date. Items must be **unused and unaltered** (no cutting, coloring, or installing).
- **Washing:** You MAY wash/condition the hair to test texture before install; this is encouraged.
- **Fees:** Refund = **3.5% fee** deducted. Store Credit = **No fee**.
- **Return Shipping:** We provide a free US return label.
- **Process:** "Fill out the [Return Form](https://www.haircompounds.com/pages/contact-hc) to get started."

### 5. Custom Orders

- **Scope:** Custom lengths (up to 30"), unique colors, or textures not in stock.
- **Terms:** 4 bundle/4oz minimum. ~25% surcharge. 50% non-refundable deposit.
- **Lead Time:** 4–6 weeks production time.

### 6. Product Knowledge (The Matchmaker)

- **Methods:** Hand-Tied Wefts, I-Tips (Cylinder), Polymer Bond (Keratin).
- **Textures:**
  - *Natural Wave*: Gentle wave, most versatile.
  - *Extra Body Wave*: Stronger wave pattern.
  - *Natural Curly*: Tightest curl we offer.
  - *Premium Natural Wave*: High-grade natural wave with a smooth, soft feel.
- **Colors:** Ask if they are looking for "Natural", "Ash/Cool", "Blond/Bleach", or "Red/Gold".
